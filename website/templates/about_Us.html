About Our Safety and Maintenance Web App

Welcome to the Safety and Maintenance Web App designed for Durban University of Technology (DUT). Our app is dedicated to ensuring the safety and well-being of students, faculty, and staff, while also providing efficient maintenance services across the campus.

We understand the importance of maintaining a safe and conducive environment for learning and working, which is why our app aims to streamline safety reporting and maintenance requests.

Our Mission

Our mission is to enhance safety measures and facilitate maintenance operations at DUT by providing a user-friendly platform for reporting safety concerns and requesting maintenance services.

Our Vision

We envision a campus environment where safety is paramount, and maintenance issues are promptly addressed, contributing to an optimal learning and working experience for everyone at DUT.
